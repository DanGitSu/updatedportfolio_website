Future Me

Apart from being successful in the job and career that I pursue. I would love to participate in chess competitions. My love for cars and motorcycles will probably lead me to buy a few and make them my own. Ultimately I just want to be happy and be able to pursue the things I want and love freely.

I hope that future me has kept connections with my family and friends strong as my company is just as if not more important than my possessions.

I hope that future me takes better care of my health and seeks to be less reckless in deciding whether or not to do something, go somewhere or say something.
